Mission of UNICEF (in brief)

UNICEF in action examines what the organization and its partners are doing in the area of immunization to ensure every child’s right to be protected against preventable diseases and to meet the Millennium Development Goals of reducing child mortality, improving maternal health and reversing the spread of killer diseases.

A focus is placed on examining the goals, progress and challenges in the key areas of:

 

  • Expanding immunization coverage
  • strengthening cold chain and logistics
  • Eradicating Polio
  • Eliminating MNT
  • Vaccinating hard-to-reach communities
  • Securing vaccine supply
  • Reducing measles mortality
  • Engaging Communities


"The website provides selected information on immunization when compared to the WHO website. It is less comprehensive and covers mainly general immunisation topics, less documents and the same links as presented on the WHO page are available."
