Vaccine Supply and Procurement

The purchase of vaccines is complex and requires a specialized knowledge and a precise, often varying approach which health specialists are not always aware of.

 

There are many companies producing vaccines but only a few meet internationally recognized standards of safety and effectiveness.

 

The WHO therefore recommends vaccine purchases through well established procurement agencies.
