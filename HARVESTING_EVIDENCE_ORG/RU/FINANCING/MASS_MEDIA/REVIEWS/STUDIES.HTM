Studies Included in Grilli 2002

Twenty studies were included in Grilli 2002.

 

The Study designs searched were randomised controlled trials, before and after studies and interrupted time series. However, only interrupted time series were found and included in the study.

 

The geographic scope was limited to HIC (USA, Europe and Australia) except for Argentina

 

Time frame of the studies: published between 1979 and 1998.

 

Paunio 1991 looked at the effect of an immunisation promoting campaign in Australia on immunisation coverage in children 14 to 28 months of age (measles and rubella). A regression analysis was performed taking time trend into account and reporting statistically significant p values.
