Studies Included in Jacobson 2009

Fourty seven studies were included in Jacobson 2009.

 

Study designs included were randomised controlled trials, before and after studies and interrupted time series.

 

The geographical scope was limited to HIC: USA (36 studies), Canada (5), Australia (2), New Zealand (2), Denmark (1) and United Kingdom (1).

 

Time frame of the studies: published between 1992 and 2006.
