Studies Included in Bosch-Capblanch 2008

The following studies were included in both Bosch-Capblanch 2008 and Ross-Degnan 1997: Curtale 1995, Delacolette 1996, Hetta 1984, Kafle, Kafuko and Loevinshon 1995.

 

Go to next review

 

74 studies where included in Bosch-Capblanch 2008:

  • policy and guidelines documents (8 reports)
  • field reports (54 studies)
  • assessments of the effects of supervision (12 experimental or quasi-experimental studies)

The design of included studies ranged from manuals and policy documents up to experimental designs. Studies were allocated to comply with each one of the three objectives of the review according to their design.

 

 

Geographical scope: most of the field stuides took place in Africa, followed by Asia, Ameica and Oceania. The review focused on LMIC.

 

The time frame of the studies ranged from 1979 up to 2006.

Studies Included in Ross-Degnan 1997

Studies included in this site from Ross-Degnan 1997 were those experimental studies of good quality design, as defined by the authors, addressing the following interventions (30 studies):

  • workshops / training (12 studies)
  • community case management (12 studies)
  • administrative / managerial strategies (6 studies)

(Group process strategies and essential drugs or drugs supply were left out).

 

Geographical scope: Asia (59% of studies), Africa (31% of studies) and the rest from Latin America.

 

Time frame: the publication year of studies ranged from 1978 up to 1996.
