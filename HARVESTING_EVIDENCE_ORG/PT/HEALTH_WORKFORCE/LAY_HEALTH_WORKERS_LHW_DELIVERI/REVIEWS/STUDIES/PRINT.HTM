Studies Included in Lewin 2005

Forty three studies were included in the whole review and 12 of them have been considered specially relevant to this site: those related to immunisation outcomes, breast feeding promotion and breast cancer screening promotion.

 

Studies dealing  with immunisation were: Barnes 1999, Johnson 1993 and Krieger 2000; with breast-feeding promotion: Caulfield 1998, Dennis 2002, Haider 2000, Morrell 2000 and Morrow 1999; and with breast cancer: Andersen 2000, Duan 2000, Hoare 1994 and Sung 1997.

 

The design of included studies was randomised controlled trials (10 of them were cluster randomised).

 

Geographical scope for the whole review: most of the studies were carried out in the USA (24 studies), followed by Canada (4) and UK (4). Single studies were included from each of these countries: Australia, Bangladesh, Ethiopia, India, Ireland, Mexico, New Zealand, South Africa, Taiwan, and Tanzania and Thailand.

 

The three studies relevant to immunisation were conducted in low income population in urban USA (Barnes 1999 and Krieger 2000) and in urban Ireland (Johnson 1993).

 

The time frame of the 43 included studies ranged from 1974 up to 2002.
