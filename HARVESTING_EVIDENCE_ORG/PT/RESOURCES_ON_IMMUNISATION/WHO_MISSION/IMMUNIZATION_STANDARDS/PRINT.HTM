WHO Immunization Standards

Immunization Standards

Norms and standards on production, manufacturing, licensing and control of:

 

  • Biological products (e.g. vaccines, blood products...)
  • Technologies (based on scientific information, international consensus, information exchange with national, regional authorities, private and public sector, manufacturers, laboratories)

 

with the aim to assure safety and quality of biological medicines.

More Information

Reading material:

 

  • Technical reports of the WHO Expert Committee on Biological Standardization (ECBS) (e.g. vaccination standards for various diseases)
  • Information about upcoming events of e.g. WHO working groups (e.g. on pneumoccoccal vaccines)
  • Guidelines
  • Publications
  • Meeting reports
