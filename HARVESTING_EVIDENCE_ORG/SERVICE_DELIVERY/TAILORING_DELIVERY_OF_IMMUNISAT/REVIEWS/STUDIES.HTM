Studies Included in Batt 2004

One study was included in both reviews: Ekunwe 1984.

 

Go to next review

 

34 studies were finally included in Batt 2004: 24 presented data on effectiveness of interventions in immunisation coverage, 15 on costs and 5 on cost-effectiveness. One of the studies (Diene 2003) was considered as two reports (one for chapters 2 and 6, and another one in pages 1 to 30).

 

Study designs included single time series (13 studies), mulitple time series (10) and multi-group design in one time period (1). One of the studies assessed coverage using the WHO cluster sampling design.

 

The geographical scope included Africa (11 interventions, 9 of them in West Africa), Latin America (7), Middle East (3), South East Asia (2) and the Pacific (1).

 

Time frame of the studies: published between 1984 and 2003.

Studies Included in Pegurri 2005

40 studies were finally included in Pegurri 2005. From the initial list of 60 studies (before excluding those of poor quality): 52 presented data on effectiveness of interventions in immunisation coverage, 7 on costs and 3 on cost-effectiveness.

 

Study designs included single time series (37 studies); randomised controlled trials (3). (Incomplete description provided in the review).

 

The geographical scope included Africa (10 interventions), South East Asia (6) and Latin Americal (6). Most of the studies were sub-national, involving particular regions or districts.


Time frame of the studies: published between 1976 and 2001.
