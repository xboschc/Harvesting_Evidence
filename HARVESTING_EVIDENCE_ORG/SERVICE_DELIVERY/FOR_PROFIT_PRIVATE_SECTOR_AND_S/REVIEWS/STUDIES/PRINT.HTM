Studies Included in Patouillard 2007

 

14 studies reporting 14 interventions relevant to this topic were included.

 

Study designs included 'impact evaluations': studies "documenting changes over time (pre-post), comparing an intervention area with a control area (controlled), or comparing changes over time in an intervention area with changes over time in a control area (pre-post with control), with or without randomisation".

 

The geographical scope was LMIC in Africa, Latin America and Asia.

 

Time frame of the studies: published between 1990 and 2005.
