Studies Included in Ejemot 2008

Several studies were included in both reviews: Han 1989, Luby 2004 and Stanton 1987.

 

Luby 2004 met six out of seven study design quality criteria (assessed in McDonald 2008) and provided sound epidemiological evidence of effects.

 

Go to next review

 

Ejemot 2008 included 14 studies.

 

Study designs: in Ejemot 2008 all but one of the included studies were cluster randomised controlled trials. One study was a controlled trial in which individual AIDS patients were randomised.

 

Geographical scope: eight studies were done in HIC. Five were conducted in Asia and one in Africa.

 

Time frame: studies included in the review were published between 1981 and 2007.

Studies Included in McDonald 2008

McDonald 2008 included 19 studies. 

 

Study designs: studies were randomised controlled trials, clinical controlled trials, controlled before and after studies and interrupted time series analyses. Seven of the 13 included studies that focused on behavior change interventions, used controlled before and after designs and six were randomized controlled trials.

 

Geographical scope: seven of the 13 included studies that focused on behavior change interventions were conducted in Asia. Three were done in Africa and one was done in Latin America. For the remaining two studies the setting was not specified in the review.

 

Time frame: the studies discussed in this summary were published between 1987 and 2004.
