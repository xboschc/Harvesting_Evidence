USAID

Type: Maps

 

Scope: All countries

 

Indicators: Primary and secondary (population, governance, health financing, service delivery, human resources and pharmaceutical) indicators.

 

Brief Description: The webpage provides links to a wealth of data in the form of maps, charts, fact sheets, data sets with specific country information given for a wide selection of primary and secondary (population, governance, health financing, service delivery, human resources and pharmaceutical) indicators.

 

  • Data: individual country report data sheets or the creation of own custom data set by accessing data for multiple countries and multiple indicators are available in downloadable format into Excel and Word.Key health system indicators can be looked at.
  • Charts: bar charts, scatter plots and time series can be created according to country and the above primary and secondary indicators.
  • Factsheets: are downloadable per country covering a summary description of the above listed primary and secondary data.

 

The aim of the Health Systems 20/20, USAID’s global health project page is to strengthen health systems by using an integrated approach to address the financing, governance, operational, and capacity constraints to the use of life-saving priority health services.
